Less admin. More time.

What we automate.

Every business has a handful of processes that eat more time than they should. We fix them on the tools you already run on: WhatsApp, Telegram, Gmail, Slack, Discord.

01

Invoicing & Billing

The problem

Someone still creates invoices by hand and chases payments one by one. Receipts pile up until it's time to reconcile them at month-end.

The fix

An invoice generates automatically the moment an order or project closes. Reminders go out on schedule, and everything syncs straight to your accounting tool.

The result

You get paid faster, with fewer mistakes and no chasing.

02

Data Entry & Reporting

The problem

The same numbers get typed into three different systems, and reports get built by hand every week.

The fix

Data syncs automatically between the platforms you use. Reports generate on a schedule instead of a to-do list.

The result

That's hours back every week, and an end to copy-paste mistakes.

03

Customer Onboarding & Follow-ups

The problem

New customers fall through the cracks. Follow-ups get forgotten when things get busy.

The fix

A welcome sequence goes out automatically over WhatsApp, Telegram, or email, and your team gets a nudge the moment a follow-up is due.

The result

Customers stop waiting on someone to remember them.

04

Order & Inventory Tracking

The problem

Stock counts live in a spreadsheet that's always a little out of date.

The fix

Inventory updates automatically across channels, with alerts when stock runs low.

The result

Stockouts drop, and reconciliation stops eating an afternoon each month.

05

Internal Approvals & Admin Requests

The problem

Approval requests get buried in email or chat, and it's unclear who's supposed to sign off.

The fix

Requests move through a structured approval flow in Slack, Discord, or WhatsApp, with automatic routing and reminders built in.

The result

Decisions move faster, and there's a clear record of who approved what.

06

Project & Task Management

The problem

Tasks and meeting notes live scattered across chats, spreadsheets, and someone's memory. Nothing is tracked until something gets missed.

The fix

A shared task board lives right inside WhatsApp or Telegram. Tasks get assigned, meetings get logged, and every update lands with the right person automatically.

The result

Your team always knows what's due, what's done, and what's next, without opening another app.
